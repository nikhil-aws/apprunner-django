Find Us

Ashford TTC is located on Woodthorpe Road, in Ashford, Middlesex, TW15 3JX.
The club is on the WR Sports Club site next to Ashford Tennis Club and Ashford Bowls Club.

Getting Here

By Road: Get directions to our club on Google Maps.



By Bus: The 117 Bus stops just outside the club on Woodthorpe Road
By Train: The club is located just 10 minutes walk from Ashford Station, located in the High Street.

Parking

We share the site with Ashford Tennis & Bowls clubs and there is plenty of free off-road parking on site.
We recommend that upon entering the site from Woodthorpe Road, you continue through the front car park, past the buildings and park at the rear where there are always plenty of spaces.
