Club Facilities

The club is very fortunate to have its own facility – the Ashford Table Tennis Centre – which, arguably, is among the best in the country.

Opened in October 1981, the purpose-built centre features:

  1. Hardwood floor

  2. Room for seven practice tables or five match tables

  3. Dedicated table lighting paid for on a digital metered system

  4. Full air conditioning & heating

  5. Club room and viewing area

The centre is located on a dedicated sports site (WR Sports Club) shared by ATTC, Ashford Tennis and Ashford Bowls clubs.

Our site therefore also includes:

  1. A fully licensed bar, open from 12 noon daily

  2. Changing rooms & toilets

  3. Meeting room

  4. Large area of secure off-road car parking

Ashford Table Tennis Centre is open to club members all day every day throughout the year (except December 25th)!

Playing Times

The club facilities are open 7 days a week, from 9 AM to 11 PM and tables are open for general practice every day.

Our tables are also used for:

  • Local league matches

  • Summer club ladder and handicap leagues

  • Club championships

  • Coaching sessions

  • Special Booked Events

Availability

A guide to current table availability is published in the ATTC Weekly Schedule.
